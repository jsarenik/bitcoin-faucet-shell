About

This faucet is sending intentionally a random amount. Currently the amount is changed on every replacement that has more outputs than the previous one.

Since using v3 transactions the received coins can not be spent until they are confirmed in a block.

Signet info

The blocks on Signet network are not just mined, but also signed and their timing is a Poisson distribution which is used in order to resemble the average timing of blocks on the main Bitcoin network.

Testing addresses

Do not use Testnet3 addresses here. All Testnet and Signet (incl. custom signet networks) use the same address format and the coins sent to your Testnet3/4 address will need to be redeemed by importing the private key (or sweeping it) on to Signet (works well in Bitcoin Core, Electrum Bitcoin Wallet or Blockstream Green Wallet).
