The blocks on Signet network are not mined, but signed and their timing is poisson distribution in order to resemble the timing of blocks on the main Bitcoin network.

Rather do not use Testnet addresses here. Testnet and Signet use the same address format. The coins sent to your Testnet address will not be lost forever, but you will need to import the private key of the address on to Signet (works well in Electrum Bitcoin Wallet).
