Plebnet Playground info

PlebNet Playground is just a custom Signet network.

The blocks on Signet network are not really mined, but rather just signed and their timing on this custom Plebnet Playground network currently tries to lower the difficulty, so exactly just 5 blocks are mined on Plebnet Playground in an hour (as of April 2023).

Testing addresses

Do not use Testnet addresses here. Testnet and Signet use the same address format and the coins sent to your Testnet address will need to be redeemed by importing the private key (or sweeping it) on to Signet (works well in Electrum Bitcoin Wallet, Signet Web Wallet or Bitcoin Core).
